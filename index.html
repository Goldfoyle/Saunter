Rules

1. R18 — Only 18+ we will ask for IDs.
2. Confidentiality — What happens here stays here, phones will be stickered.
3. Don't be a Dick — No racism, ableism, sexism, homophobia or transphobia.
4. Values — Saunter is a Queer first space, anyone is welcome but we celebrate queerness and diversity.
5. Safe Space for Femmes — Leeriness , creepiness or misogyny is never accepted. We're not into toxic dudes or bros, leave your dogs at home.
6. Consent — Consent is key for anything at our events. We're sex and nudity positive at our events.
7. Respect — The space is a private community space. Please be respectful of this space and the neighbours.
8. Alcohol Sucks — Be responsible if you decide to drink, we’re not here for boozing and intoxication is grounds for removal.
9. Community — We don't make money from these events, so help us please throw away trash, don't linger outside, don't pee outside.
10. Membership — Members will be issued member cards, which are required for entry, each member can bring non members. Membership is free, and most events will be free. Membership can be revoked at any time for any reason. We reserve the right to ban anyone who breaks our rules.
