Rules

1. R18 — Only 18+ we will ask for IDs.
2. Confidentiality — What happens here stays here, phones will be stickered.
3. Don't be a Dick — No racism, ableism, sexism, homophobia or transphobia.
4. Value — Saunter is a Queer first space, anyone is welcome but we celebrate queerness and diversity.
5. Safe Space for Femmes — Leeriness , creepiness misogyny is never accepted. We're not into toxic dudes or bros.
6. Consent — Consent is key for anything our events. We're sex and nudity positive at our events.
7. Respect — The space is a private community space be respectful of this space and the neighbours.
8. Alchol Sucks — Be responsible if you decide to drink do so responsibly, we’re not here for that and intoxication is not acceptable..
9. Community — We don't make money from these events, so help us please throw away trash, don't linger outside, don't pee outside.
10. Membership — Members will be issued member cards,required for entry, each member can bring non members. Membership is free and can be revoked at any time for any reason. We reserve the right to ban anyone who breaks our rules.
