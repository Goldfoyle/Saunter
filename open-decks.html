Open Decks.

We’re building a small roster of DJs who can hold a room: patient builds, clean blends, no ego. If you want to play, send us a mix link and a short note about your sound.

We’ll reply if it’s a fit. Keep it simple.

Techno + House
Long-form sets
No phones in-room
Respect always

How to apply

Quick + human.
  1. Link a mix (SoundCloud / YouTube / Mixcloud).
  2. Tell us your preferred BPM range + genre lane.
  3. Note your experience level (new is fine).
  4. Include your contact number (optional).

We keep the email behind a click to reduce spam.

Notes

Not a club. Not a brand.

Sound matters

We’re into sets that build patiently, stay functional, and keep it about the room — not the DJ. If you can keep energy without constant drops, you’re in the lane.

Respect matters

Saunter is queer-first and consent-led. If you don’t align with the rules, don’t apply. If you do: welcome.
